Introduction

Welcome to the Ice Dice Game, a thrilling dice game where you compete against the computer in a race to achieve the highest score. Each player rolls a pair of dice three times, and the player with the highest cumulative score at the end of the three rounds wins the game.

How to Play

1. Click the "Roll Dice" button to roll a pair of dice for both you and the computer.

2. Observe the rolled dice values and the calculated scores for the current round.

3. The game will automatically update the total scores and display them.

4. After three rounds, the game will display a message announcing the winner.

5. To play again, click the "New Game" button to start a new game
